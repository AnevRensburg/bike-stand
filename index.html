Description

This Enduro Engineering MTB stand will fit almost all makes and models of eMTB and Standard MTB. It fits 26, 27.5, and 29 wheels up to 4.0 width. There are 2 adjustable portions to adjust where the bike will be cradled and how high.

This stand gets bikes off the ground and locks them in an upright position.

Works great for

  • Lubing the Chain
  • Bleeding brakes
  • Lubing and adjusting derailers
  • Changing components
  • Washing your bike
